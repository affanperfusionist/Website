{{site.title-styled}} is above all a community whose aim is to bring real change to DeFi. We have developed the ERC–3475 standard to achieve this — making investments safer and more stable.

Unlike other DeFi projects like SnapShot, we do not only poll our community. {{site.title-styled}} makes transparent, community-led, decentralised governance possible on our comprehensive platform. Smart contracts and DBGTs ({{site.title-styled}} Governance Tokens) are used to vote democratically on proposals, revisions, updates, withdrawals, and executions about {{site.title-styled}} and its funds on the chain.
